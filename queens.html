The Moon Queen

She holds the memory of every star that ever died. Her light is not warmth — it is truth, cold and precise, illuminating what others pray stays hidden. Where she walks, illusions shatter like frost on glass. She does not forgive. She does not forget. She simply sees — and that is enough to undo kingdoms.

The Shadow Crown

She writes destiny in flame and reads it in ash. Her silence is louder than thunder. Where she looks, kingdoms fold like paper in rain. Nyxaris does not conquer — she reveals the void that was always beneath the throne. And in that void, she builds empires that no light can reach.
